# FOODTURE

## Full project name
Environmental Quantification and Impact Reduction

## Researchers
- Ian Vázquez Rowe
- Ramzy Kahhat Abedrabbo
- Joan Sánchez Matos
- Diana Ita Nagy

## Description
FOODTURE is a European initiative that seeks to drive a transformative shift toward sustainable and innovative practices in the food sector.

The project aims to develop and validate improved Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) methodologies and foster stakeholder engagement to identify effective sustainability strategies and promote a resilient and circular food value chain.

## Objectives
- Improve Life Cycle Assessment methodologies.
- Quantify impacts at different levels.
- Support decision-making processes.
- Promote sustainability and circularity in food systems.
- Develop open-access tools and policy-oriented reports.
